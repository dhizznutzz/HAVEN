01 — The Problem

Three walls most youth
hit alone.

Malaysian youth face a fragmented system. Skills with nowhere to apply them. Stress with no one to talk to. Opportunities they never knew existed.

01 — Skills

Learning with no direction

Self-taught youth don't know which skills matter. No feedback loop, no community, no pathway from learning to doing.

02 — Mental Health

Nowhere safe to talk

Stigma around mental health means most students suffer silently. Counselors are scarce. Peers don't know what to say.

03 — Opportunities

Invisible opportunities

Internships, volunteering, and youth programs exist — but discovery is broken. Word-of-mouth isn't a system.

04 — Community

Isolated by default

University and school environments rarely foster genuine peer connection across interests. Study groups never form. Mentors stay out of reach.

02 — The Platform

Four pillars.
One platform.

HAVEN wraps skill development, opportunity discovery, community building, and mental health support into a single, AI-powered experience.

Grow

Skills & Challenges

Share skills, join peer challenges, earn badges, and get AI-powered learning path recommendations tailored to the Malaysian job market.

Connect

Opportunities Near You

Discover volunteering, internships, and programs mapped to your location. AI matches you based on skills and interest — not connections.

Circle

Interest Groups

Join study circles, find accountability partners, and build genuine peer relationships around shared interests — online and in-person.

Safe Space

Private Support

Talk to Rakan, our AI companion, or connect with trained peer listeners and licensed counselors — always anonymous, always safe.
